
# Kingdom Old Land

"Kingdom Old Land" is a 2D idle game inspired by the video game series "Kingdom" by Noio.

"Kingdom Old Land" is a very simple and relaxing idle game in which the player is the monarch of the island. The monarch has the power of currency. He can hire fugitives or develop his land with his coins and survive for as many day as possible.

The control is extremely straightforward. Use the a/d or left/right arrow to control the main character. Use the s or down arrow to toss a coin.

Toss a coin at a fugitive to hire them as a villager. Toss a coin at the bow/sickle shop to buy them a working item and give them a job.

There is no explicit health bar system for the player. The amount of damage the player can take is determined by how many coins he holds. If the player gets damage when he has no money, the game will be over.
